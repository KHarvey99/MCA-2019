Week 2: Notated Music

For this week's task, I have decided to transcribe The Black Keys' song Little Black Submarines. This specific arrangement was written by Anna Poole and can be accessed on MuseScore here. Originally, the arrangement is for a piano and a clarinet, but I have transcribed it to be for a piano and voice by transposing the clarined diatonically down by a second.
